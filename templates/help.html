Getting Started Guide

Follow these steps to generate your timetable.

Navigate to Dashboard → Teachers and add each teacher with a unique ID and full name.

Add all available classrooms with their room number and seating capacity.

Define available time slots for each day. For example: Monday 9:30-10:30, Monday 10:30-11:30, etc.

Add courses with their code, name, max students, and assign teacher(s).

Create departments, assign courses, then create sections within departments specifying classes per week.

Go to Generate Timetable, verify data, and click Generate. Download the result as PDF.
